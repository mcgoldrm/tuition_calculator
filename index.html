About the Fees

Consolidated Fee: 20% of tuition (min. $50). Non-refundable after the first week of classes.

Major Facility Fee: Funds capital facilities projects. Non-refundable.

Technology Fee: Offsets instructional technology costs. Non-refundable.

Transportation Fee: Supports transportation operations. Non-refundable.
